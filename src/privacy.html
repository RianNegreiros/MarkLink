MarkLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Introduction

MarkLink is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our browser extension.

Data Collection and Usage

MarkLink is designed with privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing history.
  • Local Storage: Any settings you configure in the extension are stored locally on your device using browser storage.
  • Clipboard Access: The extension only accesses your clipboard when you explicitly use the copy feature.

Permissions Used

MarkLink requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: Used to access the current tab's URL and title for generating Markdown links.
  • clipboardWrite: Used to copy generated Markdown links to your clipboard.
  • contextMenus: Used to add the "Copy as Markdown link" option to the right-click menu.
  • storage: Used to save your extension preferences locally.

Third-Party Services

MarkLink does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the extension's support channels.
